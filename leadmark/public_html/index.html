Mission

At Salvation and Light Temple, our mission is to illuminate lives with the light of Christ, leading individuals into a transformative relationship with God. Through heartfelt worship, biblical teaching, intentional discipleship, and acts of compassionate service, we seek to draw people closer to the heart of God. Together, we strive to nurture spiritual growth, equip believers to share His love and truth, and build a Christ-centered community that reflects His glory and advances His kingdom.
